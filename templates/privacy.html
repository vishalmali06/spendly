Privacy Policy

Your financial data is personal. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly: your account details (name, email, password) and the expense entries you log (amount, category, date, description). We may also collect basic technical data such as browser type and timestamps to keep the service running.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide Spendly's core features — storing your expenses, generating summaries, and showing spending patterns over time. We do not sell your personal information or share it with advertisers.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, after which it will be removed from our active systems.

Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of third-party providers for hosting and essential infrastructure. These providers process data only on our behalf and under contractual obligations to protect it. We do not integrate with advertising or analytics networks that profile users.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights over your data, reach out to us at privacy@spendly.example. We aim to respond within a reasonable timeframe.
